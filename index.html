个人简介

Tin Lun LAM is an Associate Professor - Presidential Young Fellow at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, an Executive Deputy Director of the National-local Joint Engineering Laboratory of Robotics and Intelligent Manufacturing, a Director of the Center for Intelligent Robots at Shenzhen Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics for Society, Senior Member of IEEE and a Vice Chair of IEEE Robotics and Automation Society Guangzhou Chapter. He serves as an Associate Editor of IEEE Transactions on Robotics (T-RO), IEEE Transactions on Automation Science and Engineering (T-ASE), and Journal of Field Robotics (JFR), and a Technical Editor for IEEE/ASME Transactions on Mechatronics (TMECH). He received his B.Eng. Degree with First Class Honors and Ph.D. Degree in Robotics and Automation from The Chinese University of Hong Kong.
